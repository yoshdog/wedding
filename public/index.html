      Tea Ceremony

      October 23, 2014 7:00am - 3:00pm My Tho

      The traditional Vietnamese wedding is one of the most important ceremonies in Vietnamese culture with influence from Confucian and Buddhist ideologies. It is a significant day not only to the couple involved but also for both families. Thus, it usually includes quite a few formal ritual observances. Come and join our traditional wedding to experience a “truly” Vietnamese wedding.
